Steganography

Is the practice of concealing a file, message, image, or video within another file, message, image, or video.

Web Exploitation

Deals essentially with security of websites, web applications and web services

Cryptography

Is the practice and study of techniques for secure communication in the presence of third parties called adversaries.

Forensic

Investigate a system whose purpose is to identify an attacker or an attack scenario

Reverse engineering

Is the process of extracting informations from any program or application in order to rebuild other applications using the extracted knowledge or informations.

Network security

Network security consists of the policies and practices adopted to prevent and monitor unauthorized access, misuse, modification, or denial of a computer network and network-accessible resources.

About

AllSafe

AllSafe is a non-profit club established within the National School of Engineering in Sousse (ENISO) in 25/01/2017. From ethical hacking skills to cyber technology we introduce it all to our members through our various workshops and events organized by engineering students themselves. We also help them get familiar with the whole concept of computer security.

Because we believe that everyone has the right to defend his services and protect his cyber life AllSafe has been created.
